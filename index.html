About Golden Beans

Golden Beans is a boutique roastery built on precision, rarity and craftsmanship. We partner directly with elite growers in Panama, Ethiopia and Kenya to source micro-lots that reveal exceptional aromatic profiles. Each lot is roasted in micro-quantities to highlight its natural sweetness, clarity and depth.

Our Craft

  1. Origin Selection — Direct partnerships with micro-lot farms and careful cupping to select top lots.
  2. Precision Roasting — Small-batch runs tuned to reveal the signature taste of each lot.
  3. Luxury Packaging — Airtight gold-accent bags that preserve aroma and freshness.
