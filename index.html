About Golden Beans

Golden Beans is a boutique roastery built on precision, rarity, and craftsmanship. We travel to remote estates, cup hundreds of lots, and select only the most expressive micro-lots. Each batch is roasted in micro-quantities to reveal clarity, sweetness and aromatic nuance.

Our Craft

  1. Origin Selection — Direct partnerships with micro-lot farms and careful cupping to pick elite lots.
  2. Precision Roasting — Small-batch roasts tuned to unlock each lot’s signature character.
  3. Luxury Packaging — Airtight, gold-accented bags that preserve aroma and freshness.
